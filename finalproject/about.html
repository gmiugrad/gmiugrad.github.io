About the Archive

The American Prison Writing Archive is a digital, open access archive created by Professor Doran Larson at Hamilton College in Clinton, New York. It houses essay submissions from anyone with first-hand experience of the US Carceral system, including incarcerated people, visitors, prison guards and employees, and teachers. Its aim is “to replace speculation on and misrepresentation of prisons, imprisoned people, and prison workers with first-person witness by those who live and work on the receiving end of American criminal justice.”

About this Project
